Yifei Lu

I am a master's student in Communication at the Ohio State University, where I am advised by Prof. Joseph Bayer and a member of Mobile Social Cognition Lab and the Computational and Statistical Models Group.

I am interested in using intensive longitudinal data and experimental data to understand interpersonal interactions and how (mobile) social connections can lead to meaningful health outcomes as well as how such relationship is personally and contextually dependent (See Figure1).

Recently, I also developed an interest in decision-making which can be personal health-related or social-related (e.g., support provision decisions) or measurement-related (e.g., which survey option to choose for an ambiguous case) (See Figure2).
